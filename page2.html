What is the Federal Budget?

Since President Trump's election in 2016, total spending has grown by nearly $800 billion.


The biggest budget item included in the updated (stimulus) package is $436 billion in aid to states and cities to help alleviate budget shortfalls caused by Covid-19. The amount is a dramatic reduction from the over $900 billion that Democrats had included in the original Heroes Act.
